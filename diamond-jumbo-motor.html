Diamond Industry Machine

Jumbo Motor

Overview

Motors are precision instruments designed for years of continuous use for diamond polishing. These spindles are direct drive motors for the scaife plate and are normally fitted to the customer's own benches.

Features

  • Extreme rotational accuracy.
  • Reliable and long-lasting, proven by thousands in use around the world.
  • No vertical spindle above the bench as in a traditional scaife; open top bench is more convenient.
  • Shaft and drive rotor are dynamically balanced to gyroscopic levels of accuracy.
  • Sealed for life to give many years of service.
  • Expanding nose centralizes the scaife plate onto the spindle axis for smoother running.
  • Variable spindle option is available.
